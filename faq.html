Frequently Asked Questions

Quickstart

How do I log time in Timepot?

In Timepot you can log time against projects and tasks. Projects are the hub for all activities in Timepot. Tasks allow the team to track time of the specific work they’re doing for the project.

You log and track your time in Your week section - on the top of the menu bar on the right.

There are two ways of tracking time:

  1. Automated - type your task name into Find or add task field and press start
  2. Manual - type your task name into Find or add task field and manually enter the number of hours and minutes (e.g. to log 2 and a half hour type - 2:30 or 2h 3m; to log 1 hour just type 1)

How can I add a new project?

Find Projects section on the menu bar on the left. Once you’re there add new projects clicking plus button on the top left. Edit the name and colour at any time.

How can I add a new task?

Choose Your week section, from the menu bar on the left. Now go ahead and type task name into Find or add task field. Once you’ve added the task, log hours writing the number directly in the table or using timer.

Can I delete or edit my tasks?

Yes, to get rid of a task from your timesheet, you just need to delete hours for the task. To edit task name or change the project pres the pencil icon next to the task name.

How can I invite new users?

Choose Users from the menu bar on the right and click plus button above Invite Users sign.

Trial

Is credit card required to sign up?

Nope. You don’t need to enter any payment information to try Timepot.

How does the 30 day free trial work?

Within 30 days you can use all Timepot features without any limits for projects and users. After the trial expires you will be asked to enter your payment details to have an access to your Timepot account.

How can I restore my account after free trial has ended?

You will receive a notification about the end of your trial. Click Subscribe and add your credit card details to continue using Timepot. Or do it directly on your Timepot account.

Can I invite users to join my account during free trial?

Yes, you can invite an unlimited number of users.

Logging time with your team

Who can see my timesheet?

Admins have direct access to everyone's timesheet.

Does my manager need to approve my hours?

No, but as an admin, your manager may monitor your timesheets and make corrections directly on it.

Does Timepot send reminders to log hours (complete timesheet)?

No, Timepot doesn’t send any reminders. It’s up to our users when they want to log their hours.

How can I see my team member hours?

Find Users on the menu bar on the right and click the user whose hours you would like to see. Note: you need to be granted admin role to perform this operation.

Do I need to assign tasks to projects?

No, you don't need to assign them beforehand (in advance). When you log time, first choose the project and then write the name of the task. The task will be added to the project automatically.

Does removing users delete their data?

No, the data remains in the system. Note: You can always reactivate any user.

Why do I see tasks in the new week before I even logged my time?

Timepot shows the task you were working on last 5 days to simplify logging long and repetitive tasks.

Reporting

How do I make a report?

Find Reports section on the menu bar on the left. You can now browse through the task, projects, users and date to find exactly the data you need. Results are displayed on the graph and under it as a 1) list of tasks 2) per project or 3) per user (you can change the type of the chart for projects and users clicking the icons on the top left side of the graph section).

Note: If you want to see tasks of other team members you need to be granted admin role.

How can I export a report for my client?

Click CSV or PDF on the top left part of Reports section to export your report with filtered data.

Can I change data in my reports?

No, reports are done using the data from the system.

Marking days off

How can I add a day off?

Go to Holidays section, mark a day or a range of days and click Add selected.

Does Timepot notify my manager, when I add a new day off?

Yes, Timepot will automatically notify the admin about reported days off.

Is everyone in my company allowed to see my holidays?

Yes, days off are accessible for every other user in your Timepot.
